About Me

My name is Matěj Adamec, online known as KralicekGamer or Kralyk. I'm from the Czech Republic and I mainly develop in Python, while learning C#. I also create websites using HTML, CSS and Bootstrap.

I have a strong interest in cybersecurity and networking. I enjoy working on projects with Raspberry Pi and Arduino. I have experience with managing Minecraft servers and plugins.

Previously, I spent a lot of time photographing and editing photos - it's still my hobby, but I don't have as much time for it now. In my free time, I enjoy skateboarding and playing video games. In music, I like hardtekk and hardstyle.

I study IT at VOŠ, SPŠ and SOŠ Varnsdorf p. o. and I'm constantly improving in new technologies.
