About Me

Hi! I'm Matěj Adamec, known online as KralicekGamer or Kralyk, from the Czech Republic.

I'm really passionate about programming in Python, and I'm currently learning Rust. I know some basics in C#. I love building websites using HTML, CSS, SCSS, and Bootstrap.
